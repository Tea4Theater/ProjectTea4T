About Us

What we are ?


Tea4Theatre a platform created by a group of theatre enthusiasts who understand and value this art form and the need to keep it alive. It is out of love and passion for theatre that has inspired the team to create this forum to promote theatre and to contact with groups based in and out of the city of Pune. There are many more forms of art that keep theatre alive,so Tea4Theatre is open for every and any form of art to be promoted by reaching out to people to complete this relationship of an artist and the audience.

"T" of the Theatre

Almost every theatre person is a tea lover.Be it a discussion of a play,reading,practice or break,it's incomplete without tea. Tea and Theatre has an unique relation.Infact the idea of Tea4Theatre was an output of the Tea4Theatre was an output of the discussion happened over tea and we thought of combining these two profound elements and hence the name; Tea4Theatre

This is how we do it

A play might tell you a story but what we don't get to see is the process,the thought,the intention,the commitment and the people behind it. When a director or an actor gives an insight of it the experience elevates.The Interview format helps us build that connection.For every good work of art, our set is ready.The insight is shared with the audience through our Youtube channel.Social media has enabled us to reach every home and very mind. We also accept posters,write-ups or videos to promote the play through other mediums like Facebook,Instagram,Twitter and Whatsapp.
